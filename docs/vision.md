# Probell Vision

## Purpose

Probell is a modern supplement brand focused on strength, performance, and consistency.

The website is not just a product catalog.  
It is a **conversion-focused experience** that presents products clearly, builds trust, and makes decisions easy.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Clarity over noise

Users must understand:

- what Probell is
- what it sells
- why it matters

within seconds.

Avoid:

- unnecessary content
- vague messaging
- overly complex layouts

---

### 2. Product-first thinking

The product is the center of the experience.

- visuals should highlight the product
- sections should support product understanding
- nothing should distract from the core offering

---

### 3. Confidence over hype

The brand tone is:

- disciplined
- reliable
- performance-driven

Avoid:

- exaggerated marketing
- overly aggressive fitness messaging
- influencer-style branding

---

### 4. Simplicity in choice

Probell has a small, curated product set.

The UI should:

- guide decisions
- reduce overwhelm
- make comparison easy

---

### 5. Built for growth

The system must support:

- new products
- future features
- CMS integration

Avoid:

- hardcoded assumptions
- tightly coupled UI

---

## Success Criteria

The site is successful if:

- A user understands the product offering in under 3 seconds
- A user can navigate to a product without confusion
- The brand feels trustworthy and structured
- The UI feels intentional, not template-based

---

## Non-Goals

The site is NOT:

- a marketplace (like Amazon)
- a content-heavy blog
- an experimental animation playground
- a design-first portfolio piece

It is a **focused ecommerce experience**
